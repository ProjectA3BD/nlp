[JAKARTA] Indonesia expects to sign an initial deal on Sunday with Iran for the purchase of crude oil and liquefied petroleum gas (LPG), an official at Indonesia's state energy company, Pertamina, said on Friday, providing no further details.

Indonesia's energy minister Sudirman Said is heading to Iran on Sunday, a ministry spokesman, Sujatmiko, said separately.

The energy minister is also expected to join the OPEC meeting in Vienna on June 2.
